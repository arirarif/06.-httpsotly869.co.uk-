## Client site media patterns
- Domain pattern:
  - Main uploads: https://otly869.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/...
  - Theme assets: https://otly869.co.uk/wp-content/themes/woodmart/...
- Product images include multi-angle sets and multiple color variants.
- Thumbnail derivatives are present with size suffixes (e.g. -150x150, -1536x1536).
