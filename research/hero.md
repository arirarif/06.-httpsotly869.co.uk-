# Hero and Above-the-Fold Research

## Homepage hero observations
- Rotating/stacked hero narratives around:
  - Heritage 869
  - Wear Your Resolve
  - Shadows & Sparks
- Repeated CTA pattern:
  - Discover the story
  - Shop now

## Current strengths
- Strong emotional positioning.
- Clear category story links.
- Shipping offer visible in top banner.

## Current weaknesses affecting PDP intent
- Homepage promises premium storytelling, but PDP visual experience feels comparatively standard/clunky.
- User specifically asked for a more complete product view experience (Zalando-like confidence before add to cart).

## Single product above-the-fold must deliver
- Large, immediately readable product media area.
- Visible thumbnails/variant-linked media without hunting.
- Price + size + color + quantity + add-to-basket all visible without confusion.
- Clear support text near CTA: shipping, returns, secure checkout, made-to-order expectations.

## Hero-to-PDP continuity recommendations
- Keep brand phrase "Wear Your Resolve" as subtle PDP microcopy near title/short description.
- Add 3 trust chips directly under CTA on PDP:
  - Made to order
  - Free UK shipping over GBP75
  - Secure checkout
- Use same restrained premium tone; avoid cluttered promotional language on PDP.
