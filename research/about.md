# About Page Research

## Core story extracted
- Founder-led brand born from a difficult period and career pivot.
- Emphasis on resilience, identity, and forward movement.
- Heritage element linked to St Kitts (dial code 869) and reconnection to family roots.

## Brand themes that should influence PDP UX copy
- Strength and progress.
- Purposeful purchases.
- Cultural and music-inspired identity.

## Supporting narrative modules found
- About OTLY 869
- Our Story
- Heritage 869
- Wear Your Resolve
- Shadows & Sparks

## How to use this in single product page
- Keep emotional story in product description, but improve scan structure:
  - 1 short emotional intro paragraph.
  - 4-6 bullet benefit points.
  - Material/care/specs in clear blocks.
- Preserve authentic voice while reducing visual fatigue.

## Practical CRO interpretation
- Story is a competitive advantage; do not remove it.
- But story must be paired with frictionless selection and confidence cues to convert.
- For mobile, ensure description starts concise with optional expand/collapse behavior.
