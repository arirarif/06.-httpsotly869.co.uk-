# OTLY 869 - Brand Research

## Brand snapshot
- Brand: OTLY 869
- Positioning: Affirmation-led apparel inspired by Caribbean heritage, resilience, identity, and music culture.
- Core message: "Wear Your Resolve."
- Commercial model: Print-on-demand apparel (Printful currently), with interest in adding static greeting cards (Prodigi).

## Mission and narrative
- Mission theme: Help customers wear identity and resilience through meaningful designs.
- Story anchor: Founder created the brand during a difficult personal period and ties the name 869 to St Kitts heritage.
- Cultural anchor: Caribbean roots + British identity.

## Tone of voice
- Reflective and purposeful.
- Emotional but grounded.
- Clean, premium, not flashy.

## Trust/value signals currently on site
- "Made to order" (waste reduction).
- "Premium quality".
- "Secure checkout".
- Free UK shipping over GBP75 (header banner).

## Audience hypothesis (from copy + product type)
- Adults buying statement streetwear basics (tees, hats).
- Buyers who like music/culture storytelling and identity-led branding.
- UK-first buyers (shipping/currency context).
