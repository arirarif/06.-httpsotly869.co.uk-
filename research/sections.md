# Homepage Section Map (Current)

## Section order observed
1. Header/top bar with shipping message (Free UK shipping over GBP75)
2. Main navigation with Shop/About/Contact etc.
3. Hero narratives (Heritage, Resolve, Shadows & Sparks) with CTA buttons
4. Founding Collection block with collection links
5. Luxe Pieces (featured products grid)
6. Key Features block
   - Made to Order
   - Premium Quality
   - Secure Checkout
7. Foundation/story block
8. Shadows & Sparks story content + music mention
9. Footer utility and policy links

## Reusable section content for PDP support blocks
- Made to order
- Premium quality
- Secure checkout
- Shipping policy and returns policy links

## Structural gap relative to client request
- Homepage tells story well.
- PDP visual product exploration is perceived as clunky by client.
- Single product redesign should therefore focus on media, selection flow, and trust placement rather than changing whole site IA.

## Single product-first sequence recommendation (within Woodmart)
1. Breadcrumbs
2. Product media gallery (large)
3. Product summary column (title/price/rating/short copy)
4. Variant selectors (size, color)
5. CTA row (qty + add to basket) + wishlist
6. Trust chips under CTA
7. Tabs/accordion (description, additional info, reviews, shipping)
8. Related products / upsell
